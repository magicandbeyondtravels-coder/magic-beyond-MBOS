# Positioning

## One sentence positioning

Magic & Beyond Travels helps families facing complex vacation decisions confidently choose, plan and experience the right trip for everyone.

## Category

Family Vacation Decision Advisory

Magic & Beyond Travels is positioned around solving a planning problem rather than selling destinations.

The business exists to help families reduce travel overwhelm, make confident vacation decisions and create meaningful memories.

## Lead specialization

Multigenerational Family Travel

Disney, Universal, cruises, Europe and all-inclusive vacations are destinations served, not the business identity.

## Core problem

The greatest obstacle is rarely booking.

It is deciding.

Modern families are overwhelmed by information, conflicting advice, changing prices and competing opinions.
